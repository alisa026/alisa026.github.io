Muffin recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 mashed bananas1
  • 1/2 cup melted butter1
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar1
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract1
  • 1 1/2 flour2
  • 1 tsp salt2
  • 1 tbs baking powder2
  • chocolate chips2

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit and grease a muffin tin. Set these aside.
  2. Combine all the ingredients with a 1 above them together, and those with a 2 above them into a seperate bowl. Both should be mixed until they appear homogeneous.
  3. Fold in the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients. Make sure not to overmix here!
  4. Fill muffin tin with batter and place it into the oven. Muffins shoudl be ready in 14-18 miutes, or whenever a fork comes out clean.
